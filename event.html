DEFECTO

Its time to wake your inner sleuth guys. Participate in the blazing rounds of Defecto and explore yourself.

Round 1 :A pen and paper round where participants are asked to guess the suitable methods of producing casting from the images and videos presented.

Round 2 :Participants who have been shortlisted are given a cast component and asked to describe its process of manufacturing.

Round 3 :Shortlisted participants for the final round have to identify the defect in a given sample.

×

FUSE 'N' SEAL

Want to know more about Welding? Then gear up guys. This event will let you know about numerous weldment identification, parameters with a quality control check.

Round 1 :Participants must use the provided hints to determine the type of welding technique.

Round 2 :Participants who have been shortlisted are given a cast component and asked to describe its process of manufacturing.

Round 3 :: In the final round, participants have to weld a sample by optimizing the parameters.

MATICIA

Maticia opens a way for you to expertise in the field of material science with practical knowledge.

Round 1 :The participants have to identify the false statements. Based on the scientific understanding and time constraints, the participants will be selected for the next round.

Round 2 :The participants are given a problem sheet and are supposed to discuss the problem, their solution and benefits as well.

Round 3 :Participants are given a topic to be discussed upon and winners are selected based on your knowledge, delivery style etc.
